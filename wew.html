About Famous Fourth


Welcome to Famous Fourth: A Tamaraw's Place,situated in the heart of Moraytanear Far Eastern University. Beckoning students to step in and embrace the scent of freshly brewed coffee with eye-catching sweets to devour, our café is a cozy haven for students, teachers, or nearby people alike, looking for a place to catch up with friends and do their schoolwork while indulging in a freshly brewed coffee and a sweet treat.

At Famous Fourth, we take in our selection of famous sweet desserts with a twist, perfectly crafted recipes through time passed down by generations, guaranteeing tempting glamour and taste. Indulge your cravings with smooth ensaymada and spanish bread that is hard to resist. Whether you're taking a break from class or craving a quick sip and bite, Famous Fourth will be your go-to destination that can cater to all your needs, satisfying your stomach and soul.
